Fog groups

Use Group select, paint the squares that belong to one room/area, then save the group. You can reveal or hide the whole group later.

Fogged squares are red outlined on the DM screen so you can still read the map underneath. On the player screen they are fully blocked.

Left-click or drag to paint. Use Reveal brush to remove fog and Hide brush to put it back.
